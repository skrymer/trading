BACKTEST CONFIGURATION

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Strategy Selection
──────────────────
Choose from predefined entry and exit strategies (Plan Alpha, Plan Beta,
Plan ETF, Plan Money, etc.) or build custom strategies using a condition-based
builder. Custom strategies combine multiple predefined conditions like EMA
crossovers, market breadth filters, sentiment thresholds, and ATR-based stops.

[SCREENSHOT: Strategy selection dropdown showing predefined strategies and
"Create Custom" option]

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Position Limiting with Trade Ranker
────────────────────────────────────
Limit maximum concurrent positions and prioritize entries using the trade
ranker. When multiple signals trigger simultaneously, the ranker scores
potential trades based on technical strength, allowing you to take the best
setups while respecting position limits.

[SCREENSHOT: Position limit configuration with trade ranker settings]

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Cooldown Period
───────────────
Set a cooldown period in days between trades on the same symbol. Prevents
churning and allows time for new setups to develop after exiting a position.

[SCREENSHOT: Cooldown period input field]

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Stock Selection with Underlying Assets
───────────────────────────────────────
Select individual stocks, ETFs, or leveraged products. System automatically
maps leveraged ETFs to their underlying (TQQQ→QQQ, SOXL→SOXX, etc.) for
signal generation, or manually specify custom underlying symbols for any
position.

[SCREENSHOT: Stock selection interface showing automatic underlying mapping]

BACKTEST RESULTS

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Backtest Statistics
───────────────────
Comprehensive performance metrics including total trades, win rate, average
win/loss, edge (expected return per trade), total P/L, ROI, maximum drawdown,
and largest winning/losing trades. Stats are broken down by sector for
performance attribution analysis.

[SCREENSHOT: Statistics dashboard showing key metrics and sector breakdown]

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Trade Details
─────────────
Complete trade log with entry/exit dates, prices, position sizes, P/L per
trade, and exit reasons (stop loss, signal reversal, etc.). Sortable and
filterable by symbol, profit, date range, or exit condition.

[SCREENSHOT: Trade list table with sorting and filtering options]

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Sector Breakdown
────────────────
Performance analysis by market sector showing profit/loss, win rate, and
trade count for each sector. Identifies which sectors your strategy performs
best in and helps optimize sector allocation or filtering.

[SCREENSHOT: Sector performance breakdown chart/table]

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Equity Curve
────────────
Visual representation of cumulative account balance over time. Shows drawdown
periods, recovery patterns, and overall strategy consistency. Interactive
chart allows zooming into specific time periods.

[SCREENSHOT: Equity curve chart showing cumulative returns]

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Monte Carlo Simulation
──────────────────────
Run thousands of randomized variations of the trade sequence to assess
strategy robustness. Shows distribution of possible outcomes, confidence
intervals, probability of drawdown thresholds, and risk of ruin analysis.

[SCREENSHOT: Monte Carlo simulation results with probability distribution]
